Before you create a Team



Here are points to consider:

  • What is the purpose of the Team? What do you want to accomplish, track or solve by creating it?
  • Create a plan for your Team. What will it look like? What will you call the Team? What channels will be included?
  • Establish a naming convention for your channels
  • Do you have an existing Team that can incorporate a new channel vs creating a new one?


Just need to chat and share documents with a few colleagues?

What you need to know:

  • Any employee or contractor with a valid MDLZ email address can request a Team
  • A Team must have two or more owners
  • Owners of an inactive team will be sent an email notification every 6 months to confirm that the team is required and needs to be retained. In case none of the owners respond, the team will be deleted.
  • Business partners can be added to your Team. You need to send a business partner invitation to start the process. Once the invite is accepted by the business partner, a Teams owner can add the business partner to their Team.
