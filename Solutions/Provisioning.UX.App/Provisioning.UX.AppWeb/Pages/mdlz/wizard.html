A Team Site should be used by teams to collaborate from a central location, share information, and store documents. Team Sites should not be used for general communication to large or diverse groups of employees – that is the purpose of intranet sites. If you are interested in a more user friendly, dynamic, functional team site, please reach out to your local Regional IS Collaboration Leads by visiting their website and opening up a new project request. Please note, there may be funding needed depending on your requirements.
A project site is a Team Site with capabilities to manage projects, including plan to track projects, storage for project artifacts, lists to track issues, risks and tasks.
STOP! Do you need a Document Center or a Standard Team Site? You should only select a Document Center when a Standard Team Site will not meet your specific needs.A Document Center should be used when large volume of active documents needs to be centrally managed.
A Business Partner Team Site enables Mondelēz International teams or employees to work more effectively with select suppliers, customers or any outside agents in conducting business. To use a Business Partner Team Site, individuals outside our company need to sign up as a contractor through iRequest.
